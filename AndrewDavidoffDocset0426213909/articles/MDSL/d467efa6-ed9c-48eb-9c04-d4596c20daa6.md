InkCanvasEditingModeSyntax </b>
 </td>

 <tr><td><div class="indent0">[values]</div></td>
 <td> </td>
 </tr>
 <tr><td><div class="indent2">EraseByPoint</div></td>
 <td>Indicates that the pen erases part of a stroke when the pen intersects the stroke. </td>
 </tr>
 <tr><td><div class="indent2">EraseByStroke</div></td>
 <td>Indicates that the pen erases an entire stroke when the pen intersects the stroke. </td>
 </tr>
 <tr><td><div class="indent2">GestureOnly</div></td>
 <td>Indicates that the InkCanvas responds to gestures, and does not receive ink. </td>
 </tr>
 <tr><td><div class="indent2">Ink</div></td>
 <td>Indicates that ink appears on the InkCanvas when the pen sends data to it. </td>
 </tr>
 <tr><td><div class="indent2">InkAndGesture</div></td>
 <td>Indicates that the InkCanvas responds to gestures, and receives ink. </td>
 </tr>
 <tr><td><div class="indent2">None</div></td>
 <td>Indicates that no action is taken when the pen sends data to the InkCanvas. </td>
 </tr>
 <tr><td><div class="indent2">Select</div></td>
 <td>Indicates that the pen selects strokes and elements on the InkCanvas. </td>
