 <tr><td><div class="indent2">Flat</div></td>
 <td>A cap that does not extend past the last point of the line. Comparable to no line cap.</td>
 </tr>
 <tr><td><div class="indent2">Round</div></td>
 <td>A semicircle that has a diameter equal to the line thickness.</td>
 </tr>
 <tr><td><div class="indent2">Square</div></td>
 <td>A rectangle that has a height equal to the line thickness and a length equal to half the line thickness.</td>
 </tr>
 <tr><td><div class="indent2">Triangle</div></td>
 <td>An isosceles right triangle whose base length is equal to the thickness of the line.</td>
