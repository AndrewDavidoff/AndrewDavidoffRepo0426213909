EnumerationParametersBase type (section <a href="c00fefd7-7102-42cf-9f74-d3715c125c73.md">2.2.4.229</a>). It creates
objects whose ObjectType is DhcpSuperscope. It is used while enumerating
superscopes belonging to a list of DHCP servers.</p>
